TwitchPlayCookieClicker Commands
THERE ARE NO SYMBOLS IN FRONT OF ANY COMMANDS EXCEPT FOR !Commands

Buying Commands:
    Buy <Value> <Building Name>
        -Values (No Custom Amounts):
            -1
            -10
            -All
    Buy 1 Upgrade
    Buy All Upgrades

Upgrade Command:
    Upgrade <Building Name>
        -Upgrade the building using sugar lumps

Misc. Commands:
    Shimmer Click/Golden Click
        -Click on Golden Cookies or anything else that might pop up on screen (for example reindeer)
    Scroll Up/Down <Section of Page>
        -Sections:
            -Center
